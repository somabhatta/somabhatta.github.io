Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Gravitational and Subatomic Physics (GRASP) at Utrecht University and am also affiliated with NIKHEF. I actively work within the ALICE Collaboration at CERN and the STAR Collaboration at RHIC.

I completed my Ph.D. at Stony Brook University working with data from the ATLAS Collaboration at CERN. My research focuses on probing the Quark-Gluon Plasma (QGP), a primordial state of matter that existed microseconds after the Big Bang. I study its key characteristics, such as viscosity, speed of sound, and the origin of collectivity in heavy-ion collisions.
